About Us

Connecting markets through reliable global trade solutions

Milkiyah International Trade Sdn Bhd is a global trading company specializing in commodity trading, import-export operations, and supply chain solutions. With strong business networks across Turkey, Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines, the company is able to source high-demand products efficiently while delivering reliable and customized trade solutions for clients worldwide.

As a proud member of MATRADE Malaysia, Milkiyah International Trade continues to strengthen its regional and international presence through efficient logistics and procurement services. The company also operates warehouses in both Malaysia and Indonesia, enhancing its capabilities in distribution, storage, and cross-border trade management.
